The Introduction

Food, sleep and shelter are the basic needs for humans. But there is another need which is a mandatory need for anybody after a certain point of time say at 23-24 years i.e Job. Without food, we suffer from hunger. Without sleep, we suffer from restlessness. Without shelther we suffer from sun,rain etc. And without job, we suffer from humiliation and hard life. So, taking the need of jobs for the people into consideration, we have worked on a project that benefits a particular section of jobseekers those who are seeking for the faculty jobs in educational institutions. Our project also benefits the educational institutions who are in search of faculty. The title of our project is "Faculty Vacancy Website"

The Work

Our project works like this. We provide with all the list of opportunities in institutions in our college. The jobseekers can come into the website, choose the region where they are seeking for the job, also the type of role they would like to play in the institution and the sector which they would like work in like Btech, MBBS etc. And then they describe their qualification, years of experience and also can upload a demo video of how they teach. After that, this complete information reaches to the institution they work in. This is how it works for jobseekers. For institutions, it's even more simple that they mention the role which is vacant and post it. With that, the interested jobseeker can reach them. This is how our problems works.

The Goal

Our main aim is to provide all the faculty jobseekers with the list of all the opportunities in the institutions. Today, inspite of the incredible talent, many people are not able to find the jobs. Similarly there are institutions with vacancies. Our project helps both of them the jobseekers to find the jobs and also the institutions to find the jobseekers.
